How the cap works

Your quote is flat and capped before any work. Accepting it pre-authorizes the agent to run autonomously within that budget — it can’t bill you past the cap. The only time it stops to ask is if finishing would exceed the cap; then you approve a top-up or keep your cap. Either way, what was quoted, spent, and the margin are itemized on your certificate.
