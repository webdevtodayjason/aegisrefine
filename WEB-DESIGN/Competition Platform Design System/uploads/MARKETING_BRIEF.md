# Aegis Refine — Marketing Brief

**Product Name**: Aegis (aegisrefine.com)  
**Tagline / Positioning Line**:  
**"Stripe seatbelt for autonomous agents — real earning, real gated spending, real audit trails."**

---

## 1. What Aegis Actually Is

Aegis is a **Conductor-governed autonomous dataset refinery**.

It takes messy, unstructured data (Discord logs, PDFs, scraped HTML, raw JSON, etc.) and turns it into clean, high-quality, structured datasets (ShareGPT / ChatML JSONL) ready for fine-tuning and agent training.

The key differentiator is not just data cleaning — it is **governed autonomy**:
- Every important decision goes through Conductor’s orchestration loop (triage → score → route → propose → human gate → AAR).
- The agent can earn real money (customer pays via Stripe).
- The agent can only spend real money when a human explicitly approves it.
- Every action is recorded in a signed, exportable audit certificate.

---

## 2. Problem We Solve

AI builders and fine-tuners spend enormous time cleaning and structuring messy data before they can train models. Current options are either:
- Manual and slow, or
- Fully autonomous agents that can spend money without oversight (risky and untrustworthy).

There is no trusted middle path that combines real agent capability with strong human governance and verifiable audit trails.

---

## 3. Solution

Aegis gives builders a **trusted, governed agent** that can:
- Earn revenue by delivering clean datasets
- Propose and execute small external spends when needed (OCR, enrichment APIs, etc.)
- Only spend money after human approval
- Deliver the final dataset along with a professional audit certificate proving exactly what happened

---

## 4. Target Audience

**Primary**: Hermes / Nous Research builders, independent AI researchers, fine-tuners, and open-source model developers.

**Secondary**: Small AI teams and startups that need high-quality training data quickly but want strong controls and auditability.

They value:
- High-quality structured data
- Transparency and auditability
- Agent autonomy with real safety rails
- Working on NVIDIA hardware / local inference

---

## 5. Core Value Propositions

1. **Real Governance** — Conductor is not plumbing. It is the product. Every meaningful action is triaged, scored, proposed, and gated.
2. **Real Money Movement** — The agent earns real Stripe revenue and can only spend real money with human approval.
3. **Real NVIDIA Advantage** — Bulk work runs locally on DGX Spark (fast, private, zero marginal cost). External APIs are only used when justified.
4. **Audit-Grade Proof** — Every customer receives a signed audit certificate showing revenue, spend, decisions, and human approvals.
5. **Built for Builders** — Designed specifically for the Hermes/Nous community that already understands why governed autonomy matters.

---

## 6. Tone & Voice

- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Precise and technical** without being cold
- **Slightly cyberpunk / terminal aesthetic** but professional
- **Trust-first** — emphasize protection, auditability, and human oversight
- Avoid hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing"). Prefer "governed", "auditable", "precise", "protected".
